# 🔥 HabitForge

### Build habits with streaks and a GitHub-style heatmap.

A private, free habit tracker that runs entirely in your browser. Check in daily, keep
your streaks alive, and watch your year fill up. No account, no server, no tracking.

## ✨ Features

- 🔥 **Streaks** — current & longest streak per habit, with daily check-ins
- 📅 **Heatmap** — a GitHub-style year grid; click any day to toggle it
- 📊 **Stats** — habits, today's progress, best streak, total check-ins
- 🎨 **Color-coded** habits, dark-neon UI, responsive
- 💾 **Auto-saved** to your browser (localStorage) — refresh-proof
- ⬇️ **Export / import** JSON backups
- 🔒 **100% private** — nothing leaves your device; works offline

## 🚀 Use it

1. **`Use this template`** / fork → **Settings → Pages → Source: GitHub Actions**.
2. Open your site, click **+ New habit**, and start your streak.

Or just open `index.html` locally — no build, no server.

## 🧱 How it works

Pure HTML/CSS/JS. Habits and check-ins live in `localStorage`; the heatmap is a CSS grid
of day cells; streaks are computed from the set of completed dates.
